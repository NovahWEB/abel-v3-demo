What he has named

The catalog.

Around 35 amphibian and reptile species new to science carry his signature as author or co-author. These are the ones with a documented story.

Amphibians

05
P
Described 2022  ·  Amphibian · Anura

Pristimantis gretathunbergae

Greta Thunberg’s rainfrog

Abel led the international team that discovered and described this rainfrog in the highlands of eastern Panama. The species was named after climate activist Greta Thunberg —the epithet was chosen by the winner of a Rainforest Trust naming-rights auction.

Senior author — led the team  ·  Eastern Panama
ZooKeys 1081  ·  Source: zookeys.pensoft.net

B
Described 2022  ·  Amphibian · Plethodontidae

Bolitoglossa cathyledecae

Highland salamander

A salamander from the cloud forests of the Chiriquí highlands.

Co-author  ·  Chiriquí highlands
Zootaxa 5129  ·  Source: mapress.com

⤢
Described 2021  ·  Amphibian · Bufonidae

Atelopus fronterizo

Border harlequin toad

A harlequin toad from Darién, part of the most threatened amphibian group on Earth. Abel collected the type specimen in 2012 and is a co-author of the species’ formal description.

Co-author — collected the holotype (2012)  ·  Darién, Panama–Colombia border
Zoological Research  ·  Source: zoores.ac.cn

A
Described 2014  ·  Amphibian · Dendrobatidae

Andinobates geminisae

Orange poison dart frog

A tiny, intensely orange frog with a unique call, from a Caribbean forest in Colón Province. Abel is the first author of its description; the species honors Geminis Vargas for her support of Panamanian herpetology.

First author  ·  Colón Province
Zootaxa 3866  ·  Source: biotaxa.org

E
Described 2014  ·  Amphibian · Hylidae

Ecnomiohyla bailarina

Fringe-limbed gliding frog

Tree frogs with membranes that let them glide between branches. Abel described two new species of the genus in a single paper, as first author.

First author  ·  Darién
Zootaxa 3826  ·  Source: mapress.com

Reptiles

03
B
Described 2024  ·  Reptile · Viperidae

Bothriechis

Eyelash palm-pitvipers

A revision that revealed five eyelash palm-pitvipers new to science. Abel is a co-author of this study that reorganized an iconic group of Neotropical venomous snakes.

Co-author  ·  Colombia, Ecuador and Panama
Evolutionary Systematics 8  ·  Source: novataxa.blogspot.com

S
Described 2023  ·  Reptile · Dipsadidae

Sibon irmelindicaprioae

Snail-eating snake

Harmless snakes that feed on snails and slugs. Abel is a co-author describing several new species; this one is named after Irmelin, Leonardo DiCaprio’s mother, in support of conservation.

Co-author  ·  Panama
ZooKeys 1143  ·  Source: pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov

L
Described 2015  ·  Reptile · Sphaerodactylidae

Lepidoblepharis victormartinezi

Dwarf leaf-litter gecko

Among the smallest lizards in Panama. Abel led the revision of the genus in Central America and, as first author, described three new species.

First author  ·  Panama
Zootaxa 3994  ·  Source: biotaxa.org
